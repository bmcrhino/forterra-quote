        Common Questions About Our Plans

        Our Basic plan covers common household pests including ants, spiders, and cockroaches. Standard adds fire ants, rodent monitoring, and seasonal mosquito treatment. Premium covers everything including year-round mosquito and tick control plus termite monitoring. See the full comparison table above for a detailed breakdown.

        For exterior-only treatments (Basic plan), you do not need to be home. For plans that include interior treatments (Standard and Premium), we ask that someone over 18 is present to provide access. We always notify you before your scheduled visit and work around your schedule.

        Standard and Premium plans include free re-treatments between scheduled visits. If you see covered pests after a treatment, call us and we will come back at no extra charge. Premium members receive same-day emergency service for urgent issues.

        Yes. We use EPA-registered products applied by licensed technicians following strict safety guidelines. We recommend keeping pets and children away from treated areas until they are dry, which typically takes 30 to 60 minutes. Your technician will always review safety instructions with you.

        We serve the greater Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex including Southlake, Keller, Grapevine, Colleyville, Trophy Club, Westlake, Roanoke, and surrounding communities. Enter your address in our quoting tool to confirm coverage in your area. If we do not serve your zip code, we will connect you with a trusted partner.

        Sentricon uses strategically placed bait stations around your home's perimeter. Termites find the bait, share it with the colony, and it eliminates the entire colony from the inside. The Always Active technology means stations are working 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We monitor stations annually and replace bait as needed.
